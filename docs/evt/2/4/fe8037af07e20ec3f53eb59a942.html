Marc Leon Front, 49-year-old 106 Maplewood Lane resident, died at 10:25 a.m. yesterday at his home. He had been ill for six
months. He was born at Wheeling, the son of Jacques J. and Antoinette
Ornstein Front. He had been a resident of Beck- ley for the past 10 years and was employed by the R. J. Lucas Gen-
eral Tire Co. He was a member of the Beth El Temple here and a life-time mem-
ber of the Eoff Street Temple ati
Wheeling. A past worshipful master of the Ohio Lodge I, A. F. and A. M., at, Wheeling, he was also a mem- ber of the Royal Arch Masons at Wheeling, the White Shrine here, and the local B'Nai B'rith
Lodge. In addition to his parents, who reside in Wheeling, he is survived by his wife, Dora Caplin Front, two children, Mrs. B. S. Polausky and Jacques Front, both of Beck- ley; three sisters, Mrs. Abe Marco-
vich of Dayton, Ohio, Mrs. W. H. Brown of Massapequa, L. I., N. Y., and Miss Helen Front of Wheel-
ing, and one grandson. The body was taken from Cal- fee Funeral Home here to the Kemper Funeral Home in Wheel- ing last
night. Funeral arrangements in Wheel-
ing are incomplete.
